MY WORKS

ARKAM'S WEBSITE

Arkam is a communication agency based in Mons that specializes in the creation of modern and efficient websites. During my internship at Arkam, I had the opportunity to work on several pages of their website using the Elementor tool for Wordpress. Websites created by Arkam are custom designed to meet the needs and goals of their clients, with special attention paid to graphic design and user experience. If you are looking to improve your online presence, Arkam is the agency for you.

PROTOTYPE FOR INTERACTIVE ARCADE DEVICE

This is an interactive arcade terminal prototype made on Figma, specially designed for retro games lovers. Present at Made in Asia or Japan Expo conventions, this terminal allows you to test a variety of retro games before buying. And that's not all, you can also order retro consoles like Sega, Nintendo, Playstation, and more. Thanks to this kiosk, relive your childhood memories and discover new exciting games.

PROTOTYPE APP SOLICAR

During my web design training, I teamed up to create a carpooling application prototype specialized for villages in Belgium. With this application, users can create groups and share rides using rented electric vehicles. This not only reduces transportation costs, but also minimizes the environmental impact. In addition to the mobile application, we have developed a website and a dashboard interface to facilitate the management of trips and groups. management of trips and groups. We are convinced that this carpooling solution can bring real benefits to local communities in Belgium.

INTERACTIVE INTERFACE FOR ICECREAM DISPENSER

This touch interface prototype is intended to be integrated into self-service ice cream dispensers, allowing customers to customize their own ice cream. With an easy-to-use touch screen, customers can choose their ice cream flavor and add toppings. The intuitive interface also allows customers to view the ice cream as they order, giving them a clear picture of their creation. This prototype was designed to provide a smooth and enjoyable user experience, while enhancing product customization and meeting customer expectations.

PROTOTYPE APP FITBIT

This is the prototype of a sports tracking application associated with FitBit wristbands that allows you to see your heart rate as the day progresses.

PROTOTYPE APP HONEYWELL

This prototype is a connected radiator management app with a time slot setting system to allow users to save on heating and electricity. This application is designed to be easy to use and configure.

PERSONAL WEBSITE CARDRAGE

CARDRAGE is a true showcase of the beauty of the automobile through high quality photoshoots and video clips. The goal of this site is to capture the essence of these unique and beautiful cars and present them in their best light through photography and video. Thanks to the clear and intuitive design of this site, visitors can easily navigate through the different shoots and video clips and discover the diversity of your portfolio. Whether it's sports cars, luxury cars or classic cars, each video clip and photo is a true work of art that pays tribute to the automobile.

MY FIRST WEBSITE JOKO (2015)

Where the web world started for me, this is my first website that I made in my last year of high school. For the purpose of a thesis, this site was to highlight a cupcake that was designed by an Italian bakery. The goal was to create a website using rudimentary tools of the time such as Notepad++, Photoshop CS4, Internet Explorer and Windows XP (our school did not have the new versions yet).
